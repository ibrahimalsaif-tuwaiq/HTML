Things to consider before adding an image to your website

Before adding an image to your website you have to make sure that your image is copyright free. If it's yours then there is no problem here, but if it is for someone else there is two things to consider. One, is the imagein a public domian therefor copyright free and alowed for public use. Second, if the photo have a copyright you have to make sure that you have received explicit, written permission from the image owner. another thing to consider is that never link an image in your website to image hosted on someone else's website that you don't have permission to link to, because that's called "hotlinking" and it's illegal, and can slow your page because you don't have control over it.
